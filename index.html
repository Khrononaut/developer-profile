Streamlining "creased" auto-generated transcripts for further editing

Once, I did an internship at an editorial department. There, the journalists rely on auto-generated transcripts from interviews or presentation for their enquiry. These auto-generated transcripts are dozen of pages long and contain a lot of blank lines, fillers, and other unnecessary characters.


To reduce the journalists' work, I wrote a program that removes blank lines and fillers, capitalise letters after periods and i's. The program saves around 45 minutes of the journalists' time. And, it supplies them with a neat and ironed transcript which is ready for further editing.
